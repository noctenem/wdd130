About Crossedoars

Learn more about our passion for adventure, our team of experts, and how we craft personalized travel experiences just for you.

At Crossedoars, we are more than just a rafting company—we are a team of passionate adventurers dedicated to bringing you the best rafting experiences. Our story began with a simple but powerful idea: to create safe, exhilarating, and unforgettable river journeys for people who love nature and seek adventure.

History

Founded by a group of experienced rafters, we’ve grown over the years, but our commitment to quality, safety, and the thrill of the ride remains the same. Each of our guides is highly trained and shares our passion for both adventure and conservation. We believe in respecting the natural world while enjoying all the excitement it has to offer.

Our goal is to make every trip an experience that stays with you long after the river fades from view. Whether you’re looking for an adrenaline rush or a serene escape into nature, we’re here to take you there.
